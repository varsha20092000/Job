Privacy Policy

At JOB365, protecting your personal information is our top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data and ensure a secure and trustworthy experience.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services. This includes:

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone, and professional details you provide during registration.
  • Job Application Data: Resume, cover letters, job applications, and related professional data.
  • Usage Data: Platform usage patterns, search queries, clicks, and engagement to improve our services.
  • Device & Browser Information: IP address, device type, operating system, and browser data.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and personalize our services, including job recommendations.
  • Communicate updates, alerts, and promotional messages (with consent).
  • Enhance platform functionality and user experience through analytics.
  • Ensure compliance with legal obligations and protect our users.
  • Prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activities on the platform.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

JOB365 does not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted third parties in the following scenarios:

  • With employers or recruiters to facilitate job applications.
  • With service providers who support platform operations.
  • When required by law or legal proceedings.
  • During mergers, acquisitions, or corporate restructuring.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience, track engagement, and deliver personalized content. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Security of Your Information

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, access controls, and regular monitoring to protect your data. While we strive to protect your information, no system is completely secure.

Your Rights

  • Access and review your personal data stored on JOB365.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time.

Children & Compliance

JOB365 does not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18. We comply with applicable data protection laws and may update this Privacy Policy to ensure ongoing compliance.

Consent

By using JOB365, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection and use of your information as described herein.
